Meet our team

Balance Gym was Established in 2012, Led by our founder, Mohamed Assab, a renowned figure in the fitness industry with over 25 years of experience, our trainers are among the best in the country. Mohamed Assab's expertise as a bodybuilder and nutritionist adds an extra layer of knowledge and guidance to our mission.

Mohamed Elsayed - Your Passionate Fitness Coach**
Mohamed Elsayed is not just a personal trainer; he is a passionate advocate for fitness and well-being. With years of experience and a certified personal trainer (CPT) credential from the prestigious American Council on Exercise (ACE), Mohamed possesses the knowledge and dedication to help you unleash your full potential. His specialized training in Strength and Conditioning from the National Strength and Conditioning Association (NSCA) makes him the go-to expert for those seeking to enhance their athletic performance. Mohamed's friendly and approachable demeanor makes him a favorite among clients of all fitness levels. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, Mohamed will design personalized workout routines and nutrition plans that align perfectly with your goals.

Ahmed Talaat - Sculpting Bodies, Transforming Lives**
Ahmed Talaat's journey as a certified personal trainer began when he discovered his passion for sculpting bodies and transforming lives. Armed with a certification from the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM) and a background in Exercise Science, Ahmed brings a scientific approach to every workout session. He believes that fitness is a holistic experience encompassing physical, mental, and emotional well-being. Ahmed's expertise in functional movement, coupled with his motivational coaching style, ensures that you not only achieve your desired physique but also improve overall functionality and health. Whether your objective is weight loss, muscle gain, or injury rehabilitation, Ahmed will guide you with compassion and dedication, making your fitness journey both effective and enjoyable.

Ezz Elnaggar - Your Mindful Wellness Mentor**
Meet Ezz Elnaggar, the embodiment of mindfulness and wellness. As a Certified Yoga Teacher (RYT) registered with Yoga Alliance, Ezz brings a unique blend of physical and mental fitness to our team. His passion for yoga and meditation has allowed him to help clients find balance and inner peace amidst life's challenges. With certifications from the Pilates Method Alliance (PMA) and training in Functional Movement Screen (FMS), Ezz seamlessly integrates various disciplines to create personalized programs that cater to your specific needs. Whether you seek stress relief, increased flexibility, or a toned physique, Ezz will be your mindful wellness mentor, guiding you towards a harmonious mind-body connection.
